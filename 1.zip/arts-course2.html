Sociology

Welcome to the Sociology Department at Florida Arts College! Our department explores the complexities of human society, culture, and social behavior. Through our diverse courses, we aim to provide students with insights into the structures and dynamics that shape our world.

Course Description

The Sociology course delves into the study of human societies, social relationships, and interactions. It examines various aspects of society, such as social norms, institutions, and patterns of behavior. Students will explore topics like social inequality, deviance, social change, and more.
