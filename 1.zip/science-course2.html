Mathematics

Welcome to the Mathematics Department at Florida Arts College! Our department is dedicated to exploring the world of numbers, patterns, and problem-solving. Through our rigorous courses, we aim to develop students' mathematical thinking and analytical skills.

Our dedicated faculty members are passionate about sharing their love for mathematics and guiding students through complex concepts. Whether you're interested in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, or mathematical modeling, our courses provide a solid foundation for further studies and various career paths.

Why Choose Mathematics at Florida Arts College?

  • Engaging in mathematical theory and real-world applications.
  • Opportunities for research and participation in mathematical competitions.
  • Development of problem-solving and logical thinking skills.
  • Preparation for careers in academia, finance, data analysis, and more.
  • Access to workshops and seminars by renowned mathematicians.

Courses Offered

  • Calculus and Analysis
  • Linear Algebra
  • Probability and Statistics
  • Number Theory
