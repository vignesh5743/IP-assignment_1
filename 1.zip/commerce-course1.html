Commerce

Welcome to the Commerce Department at Florida Arts College! Our department offers a wide range of courses that provide insights into the world of business, economics, and finance. Through our comprehensive curriculum, we aim to prepare students for diverse roles in the corporate world.

Our experienced faculty members combine academic knowledge with real-world expertise, guiding students through concepts related to accounting, marketing, management, and more. Whether you're interested in entrepreneurship, corporate finance, or international trade, our courses provide a strong foundation for success.

Why Choose Commerce at Florida Arts College?

  • Practical knowledge applicable to various business environments.
  • Case studies and interactive projects for hands-on learning.
  • Preparation for careers in finance, marketing, human resources, and more.
  • Access to seminars and workshops by industry experts.
  • Networking opportunities with alumni and industry professionals.

Courses Offered

  • Principles of Accounting
  • Marketing Fundamentals
  • Business Ethics and Corporate Social Responsibility
  • Financial Management
